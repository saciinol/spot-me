Application Manual

    Step 1: Select categories

  • - Choose Categories in the navigation menu below
  • - Start form checking by selecting Categories
  • - You can also gain some exercise terminlogy knowledge from Terminologies and advice from Tips

    Step 2: Select the exercise

  • - Choose a body part you want to target (chest, shoulders, etc.)
  • - Select from list of exercises

    Step 2: Position the camera

  • - Position the camera so as to ensure that it is no more than 6.5ft away
  • - Ensure that your face and the body parts/joints that will be moving to perform the exercise are visible to the camera

    Step 3: Start the exercise

  • - Start performing the exercise
  • - Monitor the video feed to get a good view of your form

    Step 4: Monitor exercise data

  • - View your form analysis and count of reps performed, and listen to the voice critique

    Step 5: Make adjustments

  • - Based on the app's feedback, adjust your form or movement as necessary using the previously monitored data.

    Step 6: Save your progress

  • - Save your form improvement progress by clicking the "+" (add set) button

    Step 7: View your progress

  • - See your improvement by checking the graph of your workouts and list of successful repetitions
